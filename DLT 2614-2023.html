Scope of Application

This document specifies the security protection requirements for different levels of network security protection objects in the power industry, including overall requirements and security protection requirements for second to fourth level power monitoring systems and management information systems, which are divided into general security requirements and extended security requirements. This document is applicable to guide power enterprises in carrying out the construction and rectification of network security level protection, and provide a basis for the evaluation of network security level protection in the power industry.
